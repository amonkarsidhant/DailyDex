# Creator — Long-form Video

Turn one research pack into a complete spoken video script (900–1300 words).

## Sections (label each)
- **COLD OPEN** — 2–3 sentences stating the stakes; hook before the title card.
- **BEAT 1 / BEAT 2 / BEAT 3** — the story in three moves. Each beat: a spoken
  paragraph + a `[B-ROLL: …]` note.
- **DEMO** — a concrete walkthrough the viewer can reproduce, with on-screen steps.
- **OUTRO** — the takeaway + one clear call to action.

## Rules
- Conversational but technically precise. Real numbers, real names.
- Earn each minute — no filler, no recap padding. No banned phrases.
- Output only the script.

Canonical spec mirrored by `studio.py:_prompt_video` and the `video` skill.

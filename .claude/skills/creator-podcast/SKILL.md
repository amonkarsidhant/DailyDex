# Creator — Podcast Script

Turn one research pack into a ~5 minute two-host dialogue.

## Format
- Alternating `HOST A:` / `HOST B:` lines.
- **Host A** frames the news and the facts.
- **Host B** pushes on what it actually means for builders — real questions,
  clarifications, and at least one genuine disagreement.
- End on a concrete "try this" the listener can act on.

## Rules
- Natural chemistry, no filler, no host reading bullet points aloud.
- Ground every claim in the research; cite the number or source inline.
- No banned phrases. Output only the script.

Canonical spec mirrored by `studio.py:_prompt_podcast` and the `podcast` skill.
